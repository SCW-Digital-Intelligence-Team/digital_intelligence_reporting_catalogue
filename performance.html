Enterprise Reporting Catalogue – Alpha

Performance

A set of dashboards providing detailed analysis of all areas of provider activity. They have been developed to support customers in their statutory customer assurance obligations and for monitoring and understanding provider performance.

The Performance portfolio is split into three reporting types:

Public Reports contain validated data extracted from various reporting systems. Public Performance data may be used for external customers and for analysis.
Provisional Provisional data is extracted from various reporting systems. It has a provisional status as the data changes when provider trusts make updates to their live operational systems which can happen until the set deadline date.
Self-Service Self-service reports are completely user-configurable & utilise a web-based versions of Excel. They enable users to create their own reporting using online pivot tables to select any configuration of the available data.

The Performance portfolio can be accessed by following this link.

Data Sources

The data sources for the Performance Reports are typically national public datasets unless otherwise stated.

Paused due to COVID-19

Due to the coronavirus illness (COVID-19) and the need to release capacity across the NHS to support the response, NHS England have paused the collection and publication of some official statistics. NHS England will review ahead of 2021/22 Q2 to establish if further collections should be reinstated or paused.

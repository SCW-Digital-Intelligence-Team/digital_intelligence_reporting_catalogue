Enterprise Reporting Catalogue – Alpha

Coronavirus (COVID-19)

This page contains reports that have been designed to complement existing national reporting and seeks to provide localised information relevant to systems.

The Covid-19 Enterprise Dashboard provides insight through the provision of trend analysis and reporting of the current situation across a number of metrics of interest in a continued effort to support an understanding of the demand being placed on local systems as a result of the Coronavirus pandemic. The Coronavirus (COVID-19) Portfolio page can be accessed by following this link.

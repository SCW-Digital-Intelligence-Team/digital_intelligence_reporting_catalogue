Contract Management Solution

The Contract Management Solution (CMS) provides a suite of reports and dashboards that inform a view of provider’s activity and cost performance. The enterprised solution presents the data in a visually appealing format thus enabling better interpretation of the performance thus leading to improved decision-making.

The Contract Management Solution portfolio can be accessed by following this link.

Data Sources

The data source for the Contract Monitoring Solution Portfolio is the Service Level Agreement Monitoring (SLAM) dataset.

Product Owner

The first product owner for Contract Management Solution reports is Enohi Odogu: enohi.odogu@nhs.net.

The second product owner for Contract Management Solution reports is Ian Price: ian.price@nhs.net.

Further Information

For further information or if you have any questions about the Contract Management Solution reports please contact scwcsu.cms@nhs.net.
